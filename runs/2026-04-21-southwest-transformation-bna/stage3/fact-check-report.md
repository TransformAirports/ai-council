## Suspected Errors (1)

### E1. "36% of airport operating revenue" attributed to Southwest specifically

**Location:** Section III.h
**Draft text:** "Exercising it against the carrier that employs up to 950 people in Nashville, pays 36% of airport operating revenue, and is simultaneously investing $53 million in a lounge is a political decision, not a legal one."

**Problem:** The Economist brief Key Finding 7 states: "airlines represent only 36% of MNAA operating revenue, with 64% derived from non-airline sources." This is *all* signatory airlines combined, not Southwest alone. Southwest's specific share of MNAA operating revenue is not disclosed in any brief. Attributing the 36% to Southwest is a material misattribution.

**Action in final draft:** Revised to "the carrier whose landing fees and rents underpin the airline share of MNAA operating revenue" — a defensible, carefully bounded restatement.
